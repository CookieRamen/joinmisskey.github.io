Reasons to begin Misskey

Customizable Interface

MisskeyのWebクライアントはあなたの好みに合わせて自在に設定できます。

Home and Deck (Desktop)

大きいタイムラインが中央に1つある「ホーム」と、カラムを配置し複数のタイムラインを見られる「デッキ」とを利用できます。

Widgets

小さなウィジェットは様々な情報を表示し、自由に並べ替えることができます。

自分好みに調整

多岐にわたるオプションがあり、操作や表示を細かく変更できます。

Many features

Abilities of Misskey

See the usage in wiki

A post can contain 1000 characters

You can configure privacy range of your post finely

You can hide your improper messages or images

Text Formatting

Let's decorate your posts and profile! Formatting by Misskey Flavored Markdown is available.

Reactions

Let's express your sympathy for posts easily! You can choose 10 kinds of emoticon and attach to posts.

Poll

Let's ask opinions! Let's ask opinions to everyone. You can create up to 10 choices.

Drive

Save your photos and share it again! You can manage pictures you attached to posts in your drive.

Gaming

Let's play together! You can battle with other users.
In Reversi, users' icon show as stones and you can play with customed rule.

Independence and Sociality

You can determine an owner by your intention, or you can manage your own instance.

Fediverse

Misskey and other SNS connect each other because it exists in the Fediverse (universe composed of various SNS.)

Easy server building

It's easy to construct a Misskey server.
